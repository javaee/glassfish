View JMS Physical Destination Statistics

The View JMS Physical Destination Statistics page displays information about the use of a JMS physical destination.

In addition to the name and type of the destination, the page provides detailed information about the following:

  • Numbers of producers and consumers

  • Numbers and sizes of messages handled by the destination

  • Disk utilization
